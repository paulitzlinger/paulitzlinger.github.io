## Grid Diagrams of Fibered Knots

For my master thesis I worked on the topic of grid homology, a combinatorial variant of knot Floer homology. In particular I studied the existence of special grid diagrams of fibered knots. For more details take a look at my short paper [here](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.02642) as well as the accompanying Python package `griddiagrams` [here](https://github.com/paulitzlinger/griddiagrams). The full version of my master thesis can also be accessed [here](https://utheses.univie.ac.at/detail/78069).

Using `griddiagrams` I found suitable grid diagrams for almost all fibered prime knots with crossing number at most 13. You can scroll through a visualization of all results below:
