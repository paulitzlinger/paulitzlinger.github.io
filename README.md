## Grid Diagrams of Fibered Knots

For my master thesis I worked on the topic of grid homology, a combinatorial variant of knot Floer homology. In particular I studied the existence of special grid diagrams of fibered knots. For more details take a look at my short paper [here](https://arxiv.org/abs/XXXX.XXXXX) as well as the accompanying python package `griddiagrams` [here](https://github.com/yourusername/griddiagrams). The full version of my master thesis can also be accessed [here](https://link-to-thesis.example.com).

Using `griddiagrams` I found special grid diagrams for 5385 fibered prime knots with crossing number at most 13. You can scroll through a visualization of all results below:
